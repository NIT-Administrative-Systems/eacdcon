Present at EACDcon

At the request-for-proposal stage of planning EACDcon, we are soliciting rough ideas for talks. You do not need a ready-made talk to submit a proposal. All you need is a subject and a rough idea of how long you want to talk.

We'll be offering three sizes of timeslot: lightning talks, 30 minute talks, and hour-long keynote slots.

Your proposed talk should be relevant to EACD's work. It does not necessarily need to be about a technology or project that we're currently working on, or even entirely tech-related.

Aside from obvious topics like "here's a new AWS" service, something like a new JS library we could benefit from, data science tools we could integrate into our products, or a new way to manage/test development work would all be good talks!

EACDcon is also about celebrating our achievements. If you would like to provide a look into a recent project or one of your applications, that's fine too!

You will be notified if your talk was accepted by February 15th, 2024.
